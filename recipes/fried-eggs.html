Fried Eggs

A good way to start the day. The hunt for this simple recipe is over, no need for further poaching.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • A bit of vegetable oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat up the oil in a frying pan
  2. Crack the eggs and release them into the pan
  3. Add a pinch of salt and let the eggs fry
  4. When they seem cooked on one side, flip them over
