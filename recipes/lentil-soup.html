Lentil Soup

This minimalist lentil soup recipe will keep your hunger in check and your checkbook full. If needed, you can even cut out everything but the lentils and boil them in water. It's a good way to stay penny-wise and stave off those foolish pounds. Anyway, here's the yummy lentil recipe you clicked for.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lentils
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable stock
  • 1/2 diced white onion
  • A bit of vegetable oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Inspect and rinse the lentils. You don't want any rocks or dirt in your soup.
  2. Pour a bit of vegetable oil in a pot.
  3. Saute the onion in the oil until it looks yellow.
  4. Add the tomato and let it cook for three minutes.
  5. Pour in the vegetable stock and a pinch of salt. Let it simmer for five minutes until it starts to boil.
  6. Chuck in the lentils and cover the pot with a lid.
  7. Let it boil on low to medium heat for about 40 minutes.
  8. Salt to taste.
